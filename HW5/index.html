Star Wars

About

Star Wars is a ficton movie about a war in space, in a galaxy far far away, long ago. Spacecraft range from small starfighters, to huge capital ships such as the Star Destroyers, to space stations such as the moon-sized Death Stars. One of the main points in the move is a mystical power known as the Force which is described in the original film as "an energy field created by all living things that binds the galaxy together"

Actors

  • Mark Hamill as Luke Skywalker

  • Harrison Ford as Han Solo

  • Carrie Fisher as Princess Leia Organa

  • Alec Guinness as Obi-Wan Kenobi

  • Anthony Daniels as C-3PO
